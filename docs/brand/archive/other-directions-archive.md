# Cold Start - Archived Design Directions (D1, D3, D4)

Brief record of three directions explored in Paper before consolidation around Quiet Luxury. Captured as one-line DNA + tokens so they can be revisited if Quiet Luxury falls short. Research Terminal has its own deeper note alongside.

## D1 · Observatory Memo

Editorial astronomer field note. Cream paper, navy ink, hand-written timestamp top-right.

- **Ground**: warm cream `#F1ECDB`, navy chrome `#0E1320`
- **Display**: Newsreader, regular, 32–48px
- **Body**: Newsreader / Mona Sans
- **Labels**: Berkeley Mono small caps in navy, 9–11px
- **Accent**: hyperlink blue `#3155FF` for cite links, single oxblood `#7A1F1F` for "RISK" if needed
- **Move**: typeset margin column on the left running `FIELD NOTE · HARVEY · 1`, slanted black-and-white striped tape across the top below the chrome.
- **Why archived**: the editorial register competed with Quiet Luxury's own serif gravitas without adding new vocabulary.

## D3 · Deal Room Brief

Legal-pad off-white with a Didone display headline and a single oxblood "RISK NOTED" chip.

- **Ground**: off-white `#F6F2EA`
- **Display**: Fraunces, weight 300, optical 144, ~96px headline
- **Body**: IBM Plex Serif body, 16–18px
- **Labels**: Berkeley Mono small caps in mid-gray
- **Accent**: oxblood `#7C2528` (RISK chip), hyperlink blue `#2A57FF` (cite numerals, footnote refs)
- **Move**: roman numeral section markers (`I · IDENTITY`, `III · CAPITALIZATION`, `IV · RISK FILE`), table-of-contents discipline, footnote-style cite numbers `[3,9,11]` in blue.
- **Why archived**: closest to Quiet Luxury already; the legal-pad ground felt like a costume rather than a system.

## D4 · Evidence Atlas

Light-mode scientific atlas with a navy command bar and source-class color encoding everywhere.

- **Ground**: light `#F5F4F1`, navy bar `#101A33`
- **Display**: IBM Plex Serif Bold, ~52px
- **Labels**: Berkeley Mono in navy
- **Accent palette** (color-encoded source class - same idea as Research Terminal but desaturated for light mode):
  - `#1E7F62` independent technical (teal-green)
  - `#1F4FAA` independent reporting (deep blue)
  - `#C77A1E` company self (burnt amber)
  - `#A33340` press release (oxblood)
  - `#7A2024` dropped (dark red)
- **Move**: every fact card has a 4px left rail in its source-class color; small per-source classification bar chart top-right.
- **Why archived**: redundant with Research Terminal as a "color-encoded sources" direction. Quiet Luxury keeps the discipline without the chart density.
